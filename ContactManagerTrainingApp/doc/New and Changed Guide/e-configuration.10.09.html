Changes to Search Criteria

In PolicyCenter 7.0, search criteria was represented using entities, Java, and Gosu classes. In PolicyCenter 8.0, most of the search criteria entities were converted to Gosu classes, which you can extend. These classes typically extend the EntitySearchCriteria or SearchCriteria class.
The PolicySearchCriteria entity has not been converted.
