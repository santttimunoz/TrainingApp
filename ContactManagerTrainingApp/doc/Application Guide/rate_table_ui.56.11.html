Exporting Rate Books to Spreadsheet

You can export a rate book and its included rate tables and rate routines to a spreadsheet in Excel format. The rate book can be in any status. The first tab displays rate book details, policy criteria, and links to included rate tables and rate routines. Each rate table and rate routine appears in its own tab. PolicyCenter always exports dates in rate books to YYYY-MM-DD format.
To export a rate book to spreadsheet format, click Export to Spreadsheet in the Rate Book screen.
Importing rate books in spreadsheet format into PolicyCenter is not supported. However, you can export and import rate table content to spreadsheet format.
