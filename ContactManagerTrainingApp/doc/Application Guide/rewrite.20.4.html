Rewrite Key Features

The rewrite feature allows you to do the following:
•
“Change Any Policy Information”
•
“Full-Term Rewrite”
•
“Mid-Term Rewrite”

Change Any Policy Information

In a rewrite you can change anything in a policy. In particular, rewrite allows you to change the producer of record and the policy effective and expiration dates. (In a policy change, you can change the producer of service but not the producer of record.)

Full-Term Rewrite

A full-term rewrite replaces the original policy for the complete policy term. A full-term rewrite can have a lapse in coverage. See “Rewriting a Policy with a Lapse in Coverage”.

Mid-Term Rewrite

A mid-term rewrite replaces a portion of the original term and allows you to rewrite the policy to the original policy end date or to a new end date. A mid-term rewrite can create a lapse in coverage. See “Rewriting a Policy with a Lapse in Coverage”.
