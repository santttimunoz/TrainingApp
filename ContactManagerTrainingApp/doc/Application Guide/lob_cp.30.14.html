Configuring Commercial Property

This topic describes how to configure commercial property line of business.

Configuring Copying of Coverages for Commercial Property

When you select to copy coverages from a building, PolicyCenter calls the copyCoverages method in gw.lob.cp.CPBuildingEnhancement. This method uses the gw.coverage.AllCoverageCopier to copy the coverages.
