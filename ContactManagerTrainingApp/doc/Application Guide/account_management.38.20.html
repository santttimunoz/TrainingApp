Modifying an Account Relationship

You can modify an existing relationship by clicking the link in the Relationship field on the Related Accounts screen. PolicyCenter displays the same Account Relationship popup as when you add a new relationship.
PolicyCenter validates the account when you click Update to modify the relationship.
