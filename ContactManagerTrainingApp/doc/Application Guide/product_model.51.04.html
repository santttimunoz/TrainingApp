PolicyLine Pattern Overview

A PolicyLinePattern holds all the configuration information for a line of business policy line. It holds information on all the coverages and similar items of the PolicyLine in the form of coverage patterns, form patterns, modifier patterns, and so on.
The following are important parts of the PolicyLinePattern definition:
•
The name and description.
•
The coverages, exclusions, and conditions associated with the line of business.
